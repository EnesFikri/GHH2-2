Risk modifiers
↑ Increases hypo risk
  • Extra / unplanned exercise
  • Skipping or delaying meals
  • Alcohol use
  • Higher insulin dose than usual
↓ Reduces insulin effect
  • Missed or very late dose
  • Injection into scarred / hard tissue
  • Some medicines that raise glucose
How hypo may feel
  • Sweating, shaking, fast heartbeat
  • Sudden hunger, headache
  • Blurred vision, hard to focus
  • Irritability or confusion
Emergency steps
  • If conscious: take fast sugar (juice, glucose tablets), then eat a snack/meal.
  • If very drowsy, confused, or unconscious: call emergency services and use glucagon if available and prescribed.
